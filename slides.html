Objectives

  • Create a tool to map Facebook Mobility Data and monitor how decision making affects the distribution of COVID-19 cases in a community or city.

  • Quick overview to monitor decisions considering the spatial dynamics of data.

Conclusions

The spatial distribution of cases is essential to understand the dynamics of transmission.

  • quadkeyr allows for converting Facebook mobility data into raster images.
  • GeoCovidAR enables the integration of ecological components with the complexity of human behavior.
